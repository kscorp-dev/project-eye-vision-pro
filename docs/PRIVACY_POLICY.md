# Privacy Policy - EyeJourney

**Last Updated: April 15, 2026**

## Overview

EyeJourney ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our eye exercise application for Apple Vision Pro.

## Information Collection

### Eye Tracking Data
- EyeJourney uses Apple Vision Pro's eye tracking technology to provide interactive eye exercise experiences.
- Eye tracking data is processed **entirely on your device** in real-time.
- We do **not** collect, store, or transmit raw eye tracking data to any external server.
- Only aggregated statistics (accuracy percentages, reaction times) are stored locally for your progress tracking.

### Exercise Data
- Exercise session records (duration, scores, accuracy) are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
- This data is used solely to provide you with progress tracking and personalized exercise recommendations.

### Personal Information
- EyeJourney does not require account creation or login.
- We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information.
- Your nickname (used only within the app) is stored locally on your device.

## Data Storage

- All data is stored locally on your Apple Vision Pro device.
- No data is transmitted to external servers.
- Data may sync across your devices via iCloud if you have iCloud enabled for this app.

## Third-Party Services

- EyeJourney uses **Apple MapKit** for 3D map visualization. MapKit usage is governed by Apple's privacy policy.
- We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

- EyeJourney is rated 4+ and does not collect any personal information from children.

## Data Deletion

- Deleting the app will remove all locally stored data from your device.
- If iCloud sync is enabled, you can delete synced data through your iCloud settings.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
